# Community Features

Last reviewed: 2026-07-15

Status: **Maintained user reference - module availability is tenant-specific**

Project NEXUS combines timebanking and community participation features. Not
every tenant enables every module, and the experimental ASP.NET/Web UK edition
does not yet certify every workflow described below.

| Area | Typical member tasks | Important boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Listings | Offer a skill or request help; search and filter community listings. | An offer/request is not an exchange or payment until the confirmation workflow completes. |
| Exchanges and time credits | Request or accept an exchange, record completion, review ledger history, or transfer credits where permitted. | Check the amount and recipient. Ledger actions can be irreversible and are tenant-scoped. |
| Member directory and connections | Find members, send or respond to connection requests, leave eligible reviews, or endorse skills. | Blocking, privacy, role, and eligibility rules can restrict interaction. |
| Groups | Join public or approved private groups, participate in discussions, view files, and attend group events. | Group roles and moderation rules are separate from tenant-wide roles. |
| Events | Browse events, register or join a waitlist, manage an eligible registration, and use organiser tools when authorized. | Capacity, recurrence, safeguarding, tickets, and check-in rules are event-specific. |
| Messaging and notifications | Start or continue conversations and manage notification preferences. | Safeguarding restrictions, blocking, attachments, and federation rules can limit delivery. |
| Volunteering and organisations | Browse opportunities, apply for shifts, log eligible hours, and interact with approved organisations. | Approval, safeguarding, capacity, and credit-mint rules apply; logging time is not the same as approval. |
| Feed, goals, and recognition | Publish eligible posts, respond to polls, record goals/check-ins, and view badges or leaderboards. | Visibility, moderation, anti-abuse, and feature switches apply. |
| Resources, blog, and learning | Read or upload eligible resources, follow courses, quizzes, certificates, podcasts, or knowledge-base content. | Publication, ownership, file safety, and moderation vary by module. |
| Jobs and challenges | Browse vacancies, apply where eligible, or participate in community ideas/challenges. | Applicant privacy and challenge rules are controlled by the tenant. |
| Marketplace and premium features | Use real-money marketplace, coupons, advertising, or premium membership where enabled. | These are distinct from time credits. Review price, provider, refund, and collection terms before proceeding. |
| Search and AI-assisted features | Search enabled content or use an AI assistant where offered. | Search visibility remains tenant-scoped. AI prompts may be sent to a configured external provider; do not submit secrets or unnecessary personal data. |

## Safe Transaction Checklist

Before a time-credit transfer, exchange completion, marketplace purchase,
application, approval, or destructive action:

1. confirm the community and signed-in account;
2. confirm the person, organisation, item, or event;
3. confirm the amount, currency or credit unit, and status;
4. read the final confirmation and any cancellation/refund terms; and
5. keep the displayed reference or confirmation without copying access tokens.

If the visible result and your wallet/order/history disagree, stop and report
the exact page and timestamps. Do not repeat a financial or ledger action merely
to see whether it works.
